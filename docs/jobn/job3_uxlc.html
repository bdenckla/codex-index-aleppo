BHQ Job: Places where UXLC could have helped

It is also good news that the Job volume of BHQ notes 9 quirks in μL that are noted in one or more of the other three editions. I.e. these are places where BHQ reiterates something available in one or more of the other three editions. While a reiteration is not as valuable as a new contribution, it is still valuable. Indeed my main criticism of BHQ Job is that it should have reiterated most of what can be found in those other three editions. Unsurprisingly, all but one of the BHQ reiterations are not new, i.e. they were already present in BHS. (The one that is new is the one regarding the דגש in the מ of הלמענך in 18:4.)
